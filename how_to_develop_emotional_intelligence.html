How to develop Emotional Intelligence?

Improving Emotional Intelligence (EI), as outlined by Goleman (1995), involves focusing on five key components: self-awareness, self-regulation, motivation, empathy, and social skills. Developing self-awareness is the first step, which means becoming more mindful of your thoughts, emotions, and reactions. One way to enhance self-awareness is through mindfulness practices, such as meditation, which helps in identifying emotions as they arise. Keeping a journal can also be effective, allowing you to track your feelings and gain insight into your emotional triggers and behaviors.
